Best Waterfalls in the Country

Washington

The evergreen state has the most waterfalls in the country with over 3,000. The state also has the tallest waterfall in the continental U.S., Colonial Creek falls which is more than 2,568 ft in Mount Rainier National Park. Some can be accessible by simplu walking, other require significant hiking, while others can only be visible by boat or air.
